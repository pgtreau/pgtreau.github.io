Hi,
I’m a researcher working at the intersection of machine learning, healthcare, and innovation. I work on AI systems that seek solve real problems in the world. Examples from my work include improving patient recovery in physiotherapy and supporting better decisions in the drug development process.

My background is a blend of industrial engineering, operations, and applied machine learning. I’m comfortable working with messy data, building interpretable models, and turning complex analysis into clear insights that people can use.

Right now, my work includes:

  • AI-powered tools that track and improve how patients do their rehab at home
  • Synthetic expert agents that simulate how investors make decisions
  • Predictive models that help pharma and biotech companies manage partnerships

Across everything I do, I focus on building evidence-based tools that are grounded in rigorous methods. If you're working on a problem where decisions matter and data is messy, I’d love to talk.

Reach out with questions

If you have any questions or comments about my work, I invite you to reach out. If you add your email address I'll be happy to get back to you.
